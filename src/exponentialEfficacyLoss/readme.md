As a first step, the equations that work are

$$
\begin{align*}    
    \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}t} &= x\left(1-\frac{x}{k_E}\right) - x y e^{-\alpha_E x} \\
    \frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}t} &= x y e^{-\alpha_E x} - y
\end{align*}
$$

with parameters:
$$
\begin{align*}
k_A &= 1 &&& k_B &= 2 \\
\alpha_A &= 0 &&& \alpha_B &= 0.35
\end{align*}
$$
